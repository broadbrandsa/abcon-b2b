# Assumptions

This proposal site was produced as a **directional, illustrative** artefact.
The following assumptions and caveats apply and must be validated before any of
this content is treated as a binding offer.

## Data & access

- **No internal data access.** This site was built without access to Nedbank's
  internal systems, headcount data, property strategy or financials, and
  without access to Abcon's live leasing/availability database.
- **Availability is indicative.** The floors, sizes and statuses shown in the
  "Your Space" explorer (e.g. *Immediate*, *Under offer*, *Customisable*) are
  representative placeholders, not a live feed. They must be reconciled against
  Abcon's actual leasing schedule before being shown externally.
- **Figures are illustrative.** Metrics such as R3-billion precinct value,
  10 000m² of new offices, 5-Star Green Star rating, 30MW+ solar, travel times
  and the space-calculator ratios (13m²/person, 0.3 parking bays/person) are
  directional and require confirmation.

## Strategy

- **Directional strategy only.** Messaging, section ordering and the
  decision-maker framing represent a recommended narrative, not an approved
  sales strategy. Positioning should be signed off by Abcon leasing & marketing.
- **Brand assets are placeholders.** Wordmarks for Abcon and Nedbank are
  rendered as text/CSS placeholders. Official logo assets, brand colours and
  any co-branding approval must be obtained before publication.
- **Legal / compliance not reviewed.** Use of the Nedbank name and any
  comparative or performance claims require Nedbank consent and Abcon legal
  review.

## Technical

- The calculator and explorer perform **client-side estimation only**; no data
  is submitted or stored.
- Contact actions (e.g. "Book a private viewing") currently open a blank
  `mailto:` draft — real recipient addresses and/or a form backend must be
  wired up (see [`TRACKING_PLAN.md`](./TRACKING_PLAN.md)).

## Requires validation post-approval

Before this site goes live externally:

1. Confirm all figures, ratings and availability with source-of-truth owners.
2. Replace placeholder brand marks with approved assets.
3. Obtain Nedbank approval for co-branding and named use.
4. Wire up real contact / lead-capture and analytics.
5. Legal sign-off on all claims.
